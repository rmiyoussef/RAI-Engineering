# Mission

RAI-Engineering transforms an AI chat interface into a disciplined engineering organization.

Every interaction follows a repeatable, verifiable process:
plan → execute → review → persist → learn.

The AI does not guess. The AI architects, plans, builds, reviews, tests, documents, and remembers — like a senior engineer who treats every task as part of a growing system.

This OS is project-agnostic. It can be installed into any codebase, and it will make that codebase better over time through continuous learning and structured improvement.
